About Me

Hey there, welcome!

I am a senior student majoring in computer science at Peking University (PKU), supervised by Professor Yingfei Xiong. I visited Professor Tao Xie's group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign past summer. Also, I'm working as a research intern in Software Analytics Group at Microsoft Research Asia mentored by Doctor Shi Han this semester.

I am good at algorithms and data structures. I am one of the teaching assistants of Algorithm Design and Analysis this semester. I am a contestant of ACM-ICPC and I have won four gold medals at Regional and China-Final contests. When I was in high school, I won a gold medal at the National Olympiad of Informatics.

I will apply for a Ph.D. in Fall 2018. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you are interested. Here is my personal CV.


Research Interest

My main research interest is software engineering, especially software analytics and software testing. Recently I am focusing on testing and debugging deep learning programs bugs. I am also interested in programming language, natural language processing.
